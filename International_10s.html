    • Glossary
    A1: Primary assists
    P1: Primary points (goals+primary assists)
    ES or EV: Even strength
    P/GP or Pts/GP: Points per game
    GS: Game score
    KSt: "Steady" shots on goal + shots attempted that are blocked by opposing skaters)
    TOId: Time on ice in a decimal value.
    PP: Power play
    SH: Shorthanded
    SOW: Shootout winner
    ENG: Empty-net goal
    OTG: Overtime goal
    SOG: Shots on goal
    Pl/Mi: Plus/minus
    PenT: Penalties taken
    PenD: Penalties drawn
    PenDIFF: Penalty differential
    5v5 GF%: 5-on-5 on-ice goals for percentage
    SF: Shots for
    SA: Shots against
    SF60: Shots for per 60 minutes
    SA60: Shots against per 60 minutes
    PDO: Save percentage + shooting percentage (the “luck” statistic).
    G DIFF: Goal differential
    GS: Games started
    Sv%: Save percentage
    MINd: Goalie minutes played in a decimal value
    GSAA: Goals saved above average
